Form Example

MySQL as a relational database is great at giving any website back-end functionality. This means that users of your website can submit information, and your website will be able to store and manipulate that information to do what you need it to. This form example will show you some simple examples of what that looks like.

Just enter some information in the boxes below and press submit. Go on, try it!

The miracle of using a database like MySQL behind the scenes is that you can manipulate and store input data, which includes spitting it out on other pages! Once you submit this form, you will be directed to another page that will show you your information in a table.
