Introduction:

This article is a compilation of many informative sources on the topic of search engine optimization (SEO). Its purpose is to provide insights into how to harness the value of SEO.

SEO:

Search engine optimization (SEO) is the natural process of making a site and its content highly relevant for both search engines and searchers. I would like to reinforce the fact that relevant content is the key to a successful website. Without unique and rich content, you will not drive traffic.

The Google search results page includes organic search results and features paid advertisement denoted as "Ads" or "Sponsored." Google does not accept money to include or rank sites in its search results and it costs nothing to appear in its organic search results. Google does not have a priority submit that guarantees a top rank on a Google search. Actually, you can submit a request to add your URL to Google's index at Webmaster Tools free of charge.
