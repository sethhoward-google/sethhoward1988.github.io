Brooke Frandsen

<A Dreamer, Philosopher, and Spontanous Nerd>

A Nut in a Nutshell

Brooke loves the web and hopes to continue her education and professional life in that sphere. Currently, she works part-time as a web developer for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Riverton, UT. Brooke began her adventure in web development in high school, where basic HTML and Adobe Dreamweaver were taught. She enjoyed creating things and contributing to the world through basic tags, images, and lines of code. Unaware of her future destiny with technology, Brooke began her schooling at Brigham Young University as an English Major. Once she realized the impracticality and endless papers associated with English, Brooke saught out a variety of explorative classes such as Computer Science, Film, Information Technology, and Communications.

One day, after the suggestion of several friends, Brooke discovered the magical opportunities in Information Systems. Ever since, she has entered a technology and business trance as she works on world domination schemes.

Brooke looks forward to seeing the world evolve and for technology to reach new and unimaginable heights in the future. In the meantime, she will continue leaving a mark upon the world wide web.
